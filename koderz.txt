OPTIMAL PATH-TIME

Description
Traffic has become a major problem in today’s date. Now a days, traffic issues have neglected the importance of time management in people’s life . The project is aiming at predicting the appropriate route and appropriate time for travelling to a specific destination.
There will be an application that accepts some inputs
from the users such as:-
Desired Destination
Desired Date
Now, the application analysis the past data and provide
:-
The best time to travel
The most appropriate route to be followed.
Such an application will help saving ample amount of valuable time that is usually wasted due to the traffic problems that are not being analysed at all.
